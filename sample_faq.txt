Smart FAQ Test Document

1. What are your working hours?
Our support team is available Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.

2. How can I reset my password?
Click the "Forgot password" link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.

3. How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping usually takes 3 to 5 business days. Express shipping usually takes 1 to 2 business days depending on the destination.

4. What is your refund policy?
You can request a refund within 14 days of purchase if the service has not been substantially used. Please include your order number and reason for the request.

5. How do I contact support?
You can contact support by email at support@example.com or by live chat during business hours.

6. Where can I find invoices?
Invoices are sent automatically after payment. You can also download them from your account billing section.

7. Can I update my account information?
Yes. You can update your name, phone number, and billing details from the account settings page after logging in.

8. Do you offer international service?
Yes. We support international customers, but delivery times and availability may vary by region.

9. How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to your account settings, open the subscription page, and click "Cancel subscription". The subscription remains active until the end of the billing period.

10. What should I do if I cannot log in?
First, check your email and password. If the problem continues, use password reset or contact support for account recovery assistance.
